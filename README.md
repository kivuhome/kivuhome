### About us

Kivu Home curates and sells African art and home décor across Shopify, Etsy, eBay, and TikTok Shop — bringing handcrafted pieces and bold design to homes worldwide. This org is where we build the tech behind the brand: our storefront, internal tooling, and new products.

### 🛠️ What we're building

**Kivu Bookings** — a Booksy-style appointment booking platform, launching first for barbers with room to grow into other service categories.
